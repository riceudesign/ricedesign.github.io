Who are We?

Our mission is to connect, educate, and celebrate designers at Rice. We want to legitimize and promote digital design as a career path and ultimately transform Rice into a design school.

Rice Design is a community of undergraduate students interested in:

UX/UI Design
Graphic Design
Motion and Animation
Frontend Development
Product Management
Digital Photography
